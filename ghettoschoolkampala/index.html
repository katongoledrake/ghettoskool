About

The GHETTO school project seeks to undertake all measures to help teach the street children and people living in the GHETTO the art of music, photography, fashion and design, digital arts, visual arts literature theatre and music plus recreating a positive mindset through which they believe they can archive what they never had.

History

The Getting Higher Education To Teach Others (GHETTO) school project started as a dream of a young man that grew up in the slums of kamwokya, kampala- Uganda known as Mutyaba Vincent a.k.a Dream Bouy whose life story is nothing short of magical. His story throughout his childhood groomed a sense of helping the rest of his friends that he grew up with in the same community.

Our Mission

To provide a stable foundation that is rooted in developing for street children their talents through Art, photography, Fashion and Design, basic needs, formal rmal education and help they rediscover the comfort of family and human contact.

Objective

Developing street children talents through Art , Music , Fashion and Design.

Goal

Create an NGO (non-government organization) that will provide a volunteer program, child sponsorship program and also to assist in resource mobilization.
